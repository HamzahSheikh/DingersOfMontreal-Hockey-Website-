Purpose

Founded for the purpose of fulfilling the SOEN 287 assignements, the MTL Dingers are a storied franchise of the NHL. Having won 20 Stanley Cups, aswell as housing some of the greatest players in league history, such as Mo IceWalker, a website was created for fans to better follow their favourite team!

Like any other team professional hockey team, a website is made for fans to further enhance their experience. On these websites they share a variety information related to their team such as:

  • Player photos
  • Player statistics
    • Goals
    • Assists
    • Points
    • +/-
  • Team Schedule
  • Team ranking within the league
  • Buying tickets for future games
